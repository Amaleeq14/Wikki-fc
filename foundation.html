❤️ What We Support

Our Causes

Every naira donated goes directly into programs that change real lives in our communities.

🎓
Education & Scholarships

Funding school fees, learning materials, and university scholarships for talented but underprivileged students across Bauchi State. Because education should never be a luxury.

₦12.4M raised 74%
⚽
Grassroots Football

Running free football academies for youth aged 6–18 in underserved communities. We believe the next Wikki superstar might be in a village without a pitch — we're changing that.

₦8.2M raised 61%
🏥
Healthcare Outreach

Free medical camps, eye care, maternal health support, and medicine distribution in rural LGAs where access to healthcare is severely limited. Your health is our priority.

₦6.8M raised 55%
🌱
Community Development

Rehabilitating local football pitches, building community centers, clean water projects, and youth empowerment programs that strengthen the social fabric of Bauchi State.

₦5.1M raised 48%
