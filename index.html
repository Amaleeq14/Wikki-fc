Our Story

Club History

1991
Club Founded
Wikki Tourist Football Club established in Bauchi under the patronage of the Bauchi State Government, beginning a journey that would cement their place in Nigerian football history.
1990s
Early League Campaigns
The club made their mark in Nigerian football, competing in the top divisions and building a loyal following across Bauchi State and beyond.
2000s
Golden Era
Wikki Tourist experienced their most successful league seasons, accumulating titles and establishing themselves as one of Nigeria's most competitive sides.
2022–24
Nigeria National League
Following relegation, the club competed in the Nigeria National League with determination, building toward an eventual return to the top flight.
2025
NPFL Return — The Comeback
Wikki Tourist FC triumphantly returned to the Nigeria Premier Football League, restoring northern Nigerian football pride and signaling a new era for the club.
2026
New Chapter
Competing in the 2025/26 NPFL season with renewed squad strength and ambition, Wikki Tourist aim to climb the table and challenge for continental spots.
